Asked Questions

At MediCare , we understand that our patients may have various questions regarding our services, appointments, and procedures. To provide clarity and ensure that you have all the information you need, we've compiled a list of frequently asked questions below.

  • What are your clinic's operating hours?

    Our clinic is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

  • Do I need to schedule an appointment, or do you accept walk-ins?

    While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to minimize wait times and ensure that you receive the attention you need. You can schedule an appointment through our website or by calling our clinic.

  • What types of medical services do you offer?

    Our clinic provides a wide range of medical services, including general consultations, vaccinations, specialized consultations, and laboratory examinations. We also offer preventive care and health screenings.

  • Are your healthcare providers board-certified?

    Yes, all our healthcare providers are board-certified and highly experienced in their respective fields. They are dedicated to providing personalized and compassionate care to our patients.

  • Do you accept health insurance?

    Yes, we accept most major health insurance plans. Please check with our front desk staff to confirm if we accept your insurance plan and to verify coverage details.
